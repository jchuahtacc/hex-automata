Conway's Game of Life, as Hexes

Just as in Conway's Game of Life, each cell will either be "alive" in the next generation or dead if it has too many or too few neighbors. Click on cells to make them alive. Click "next" to advance to the next generation (which will also generate a json dump). You may also load a json dump by pasting it into the text area and hitting load.
